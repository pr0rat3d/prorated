# ProRated — Content Moderation Policy
# Required by Apple App Store for apps with user-generated content
# Last updated: January 2025

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════
OVERVIEW
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

ProRated is a contractor-to-contractor job site rating platform.
Users submit text reviews and star ratings about residential
job sites. This document outlines how we moderate that content.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════
1. WHAT CONTENT IS ALLOWED
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

ProRated allows contractors to submit:

✓ Star ratings (1-5) across 5 job site categories
✓ Factual descriptions of job site conditions
✓ Issue tags (pre-defined: steep driveway, aggressive dog, etc.)
✓ Written reviews based on firsthand contractor experience
✓ Helpful votes on other contractors' reviews

All content must:
• Be based on actual, firsthand job site experience
• Be submitted by a verified licensed contractor
• Be factual, professional, and in good faith
• Refer to job site conditions, not personal attacks on homeowners

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════
2. WHAT CONTENT IS PROHIBITED
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

ProRated prohibits:

✗ False or deliberately misleading reviews
✗ Retaliatory reviews (submitted to harm a specific homeowner)
✗ Personally identifying information about homeowners
   (names, phone numbers, email addresses)
✗ Hate speech, slurs, or discriminatory language
✗ Threats or violent language
✗ Sexually explicit content
✗ Spam or promotional content
✗ Reviews submitted by someone other than the contractor
   who worked the job
✗ Reviews for job sites the submitter did not personally work

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════
3. PRE-SUBMISSION SAFEGUARDS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Before any review is accepted, ProRated requires:

a) LICENSE VERIFICATION
   Every reviewer must be a verified licensed contractor.
   License numbers are checked against state databases before
   accounts are approved. Unverified accounts cannot submit reviews.

b) ACCOUNT APPROVAL
   New accounts are manually reviewed by ProRated staff before
   receiving full access. This prevents fake accounts and bots.

c) STRUCTURED INPUT
   Most rating content is structured (star ratings, pre-defined
   tags) rather than free text, limiting the surface area for
   harmful content. Free text is limited to 500 characters.

d) TERMS ACCEPTANCE
   Users must accept Terms of Service at signup, which explicitly
   prohibit false, defamatory, and retaliatory content.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════
4. REPORTING MECHANISM
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Users and homeowners can report problematic content via:

• Email: disputes@prorated.io
• Subject line: "Content Report — [address]"
• Include: the address, reason for report, and any
  supporting documentation

Reports are reviewed within 48 hours on business days.

Homeowners who believe a review about their property is
false or defamatory may:
1. Contact disputes@prorated.io with documentation
2. Request review removal under our dispute process
3. Receive a response within 5 business days

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════
5. MODERATION PROCESS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

STEP 1 — AUTOMATED FILTERS
All submitted text is scanned for:
• Personally identifying information (phone numbers, emails)
• Profanity and hate speech keywords
• Spam patterns

STEP 2 — ADMIN REVIEW DASHBOARD
ProRated staff review all reviews via the Admin Dashboard,
which shows all submitted content with the ability to:
• Delete any review immediately
• Flag accounts for investigation
• Ban accounts that repeatedly violate terms

STEP 3 — DISPUTE RESOLUTION
When a dispute is filed:
• ProRated contacts the reviewing contractor for context
• Supporting documentation is reviewed
• Decision made within 5 business days
• Removed content is logged for pattern analysis

STEP 4 — ACCOUNT ACTIONS
For policy violations, ProRated may:
• Remove the specific review
• Issue a warning to the account
• Temporarily suspend the account
• Permanently ban the account
• Report to appropriate authorities for legal violations

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════
6. HOMEOWNER PROTECTION
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

ProRated takes the following steps to protect homeowners:

• Homeowner names are never collected or displayed
• Reviews reference job site conditions, not personal attacks
• Reviews containing homeowner contact information are removed
• Homeowners may request review removal by contacting
  disputes@prorated.io with proof of property ownership
• ProRated does not allow homeowners to be targeted or harassed

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════
7. CONTRACTOR PROTECTIONS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Contractors are protected from:

• False reviews by unverified users (license verification required)
• Retaliatory reviews (reported reviews are investigated)
• Fake accounts (manual approval process)

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════
8. MINOR PROTECTION
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

ProRated is strictly for licensed contractors (age 18+).
Registration requires a valid contractor license number.
The platform does not collect or display content from minors.
